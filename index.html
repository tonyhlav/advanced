Our Services

WE PROVIDE CONSULTATIONS AND SURGERIES FOR CONDITIONS OF THE FACE, JAWS, AND TEETH

Dental Implants

Implant treatment has two stages. First, the implant is placed in the jaw. Then, when the jaw has healed, replacement teeth are attached to the implant.

Dental Extractions

Our specialist may determine that you need a tooth extraction for several reasons: advanced periodontal disease or a broken tooth that cannot be repaired.

Wisdom Teeth

Young adults in their early twenties often experience pain caused by wisdom teeth, typically appear during this time of life, sometimes needs removal.

Aneshtesia

Technically “sleep” dentistry refers to a deep sedation or general anesthesia provided by certified anesthesiologists. Resulting in a safer appointment.

prosthesis

Metal ceramics, metal-free ceramics prostheses as well as removal prostheses of different constructions are used for prosthesis. Each patient may choose a treatment.

TRAUMA SURGERY

Our trauma surgeons are in the hospital at all times and ready for an emergency. They are specially trained to care for trauma patients of all ages, from infants to older adults.
