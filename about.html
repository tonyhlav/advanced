About Us

We are modern newly build dental clinic with experienced doctors

Our Clinic History

It all began when we worked together for a large national dentist therapy corporation. The corporation was focused on productivity and number of patients care. We found we didn’t have the flexibility to take care of some of the patients the way we knew they should be treated. We were encouraged to become indenpendent.

We collaborate with 6 other clinics

Since 2010, when we opened our clinic we started collaborating with some other businesses in the dentis industry. As time passed we made very clever move to connect with other strong clinics in the same field so we can excange patients and experience between us.

Team of Experienced Doctors

Our doctors have both medical and dental degrees. They will take care with love to each patient differently. Treating our clients the best we can has made our dental clinic to become one of the best in the country. We also are focused on providing free dental consultations for the new patients and the existing ones feel free to contact us.

Serving over 200 clients

The Advanced dental clinic is still growing... with our over 200 happy clients that we have worked with so far, we expect to grow more in the next couple of years. We have a team that are focused doing analytics and finding out what keeps our clients happy so we can continue growing in that direction.
